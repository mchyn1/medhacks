Why wait for Medhacks? Let's hack all year-round.

Be able to pitch ideas across interdisciplinary communities at any time. Instead of being limited to a physical event, the internet allows us to collaborate from all over the world.

Collaborate together no matter what background you come from.

Clinical workers encounter the same medical problems in their workplace every day ranging from protocols that need improvement in urban environments to struggling to fulfill the health care needs of secluded rural populationsin third world countries. Programmers create impractical applications that go unused. Why not have a place to bring everyone together so relavent medical problems can be actively solved with knowledge from all disciplines?
